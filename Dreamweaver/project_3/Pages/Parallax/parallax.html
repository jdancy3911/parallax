Charlotte FC History

Winning the Bid

In July of 2019, David Tepper (current owner of the Carolina Panthers & Charlotte FC) placed a formal bid to have an expansion team in Charlotte. His bid would eventually become sucessful and land himself a new team by December of 2019. The bid was accepted for a staggering $325 million dollars. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic their first official season for Charlotte FC wouldn't start until 2022.

First Offical Game

Game 1

Charlotte FC played their first game on February 26, 2022 against D.C. United which end up with a 3-0 loss. Following this, they played in their home debut only 7 days later against L.A. Galaxy. This was a massive moment for the city of Charlotte as they broke the record for the largest attendence of all time in the MLS, setting a new record at a stagering 74,479 people in attendence.

End of Season 1

First Season Finish

Charlotte FC first official season came to a close on October 9, 2022 which ended in defeat however it was glimse of good fortune for the future. Charlotte FC finished the season 9th in the MLS with 13 wins, 3 draws and 18 loses. It was a great atmosphere for the fans and the city of Charlotte. It made plenty of people very excited and intreseted about the sport.
